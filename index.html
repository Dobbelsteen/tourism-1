Welcome to the Open Tourism Project Page.

The specification is the result of a public-private partnership initiated by Memori, iMinds and Open Knowledge Belgium. This Github instance allows us to collaborate on a shared vocabulay on Tourism and Recreation, the consortium will vote on the definitive toolset.
